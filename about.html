ABOUT THE DESIGNER

Yuting Min was born in Wuxi China, grew up in Watery Towns in Southern China, later came to New York and is now studying Fashion Design at Parsons the New School. During her study, she interned at Just In Case. Yuting Min gets her inspiration from anything. She usually has sudden thoughts from daily life. She believes childishness is an important quality of a designer. She always have a possibility of changing thoughts during the process of designing and reorganise the designs to make them better.
